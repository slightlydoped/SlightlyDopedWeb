About Us

Hello, we are an Indian start-up whose motive is to bridge the gap between theory and practical experinece in technical education. We have a group of fellow engineers from reputed colleges and universities across the India. We help you to enhance your problem-solving skills whether the problem is related to software or hardware. We explain things using practical and real life issues and also teach you the latest tools you need to know for your growth. Whether you want to learn programming, hardware design, various engineering tools or want to know about latest events and contests, Slightly Doped will always be there for you. It is always fun to learn and work with us. We would love to see you in our community. Sharing is caring!
